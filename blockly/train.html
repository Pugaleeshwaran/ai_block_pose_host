MobileNet V3 always requires 224×224 — your image is resized to 224×224 before the model sees it, regardless of this setting.
Larger canvas = more detail preserved before that resize step.
💡 Tips for specific object detection:
• Fill frame with your object (60%+)
• Capture from 5 different locations
• Different angles and distances
• Always add a Background class
• 50+ images per class minimum
⚠️ Avoid background issues:
• Never capture all images in same spot
• Move to different rooms/walls
• Background class teaches "nothing here"
